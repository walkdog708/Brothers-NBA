Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about making picks, confidence points, bonus scoring, and lock timing.

How do I make my picks? +

Go to My Picks, choose the winner for each open series, assign one confidence value to each series, and predict how many games the series will last. You must submit your picks before the series locks.

How do confidence points work? +

Each round has its own allowed confidence values. You can only use each value once per round. If your series winner is correct, you earn the confidence points assigned to that pick.

When do picks lock? +

Each series will lock individually at its scheduled tip-off time of GAME 1. Once a series is locked, you can no longer change that pick, confidence value, or games prediction for that matchup.

Can I edit my picks after submitting? +

Yes. You can update picks for any series that is still open. Locked series can no longer be edited.

When is the tiebreaker used? +

TOTAL POINTS on the LEADERBOARD will always determine the standings first. If players are tied in total points, 1) the NBA Finals tiebreaker prediction is used next which we'll use the CLINCHING game's final score of the NBA Finals to determine the ranking. If there is still a tie after that, 2) the standings are decided by who has the most correct winners. If that doesn't do it, 3) then who has accumulated the most bonus points.

How will the pot be split up for those who place?? +

1st place will get a lion's share of the pot. That person took number 1 overall so they deserve a huge chunk. 2nd place...depending on the number of players, will determine whether the 2nd place player will double or triple their entry fee money. 3rd place...will get their entry fee back!
